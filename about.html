About Me

Hello! I'm Kamria, an educator at SMA Negeri 1 Lasolo Kab. North Konawe. With a passion for teaching and a focus on digitalized learning, I have been honored as an Outstanding Teacher in both the district and Southeast Sulawesi in 2023. Currently, I serve as the Co-Captain and ID Learning mentor for North Konawe Regency. Additionally, I am a Google Certified Trainer, actively involved in enhancing the learning experience.

Join me on this educational journey as I continue to explore innovative approaches to teaching and strive for excellence in the field. Feel free to connect and learn more about my experiences and contributions to education.
